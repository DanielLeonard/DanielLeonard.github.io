Hey, I'm Daniel Leonard

"Im a designer who likes to take the road less travelled"

Daniel is the kind of dude that likes to be adventurous in all aspects of life. He is a Belfast based Dude who thoroughly enjoys action sports (until he hurts himself), traveling and pancakes. His goal is to help make the world better by removing ugly design and replacing it with his supremely awesome design work!

He is a UX designer with a hunger for pancakes as well as the opportunity to work with some really cool clients. He is a Jack of all trades as he does not limit himself to one type of design medium. He is always learning new stunts on his keyboard in order to create even more awesome things for people.
