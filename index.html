HEY, I'M DANIEL LEONARD

"I'm a designer who likes to take the road less travelled"

I'm the kind of dude that likes to be adventurous in all aspects of life. Based in Belfast, I thoroughly enjoy action sports (until I hurt myself), travelling and pancakes. My goal is to help make the world better by removing ugly design and replacing it with my supremely great style! I am UX designer who would love the opportunity to work with some really cool people. I am a Jack of all trades and I do not limit myself to one type of design medium, however I'm especially interested in branding. I am always learning new stunts on my keyboard in order to create even more awesome things for people.
